Search by Name

The method of searching by the name is exactly what it sounds like; you can search for a concept by its name.

 

Search Using “Starting with”

 

1.       Click on the down arrow located in the first field (qualifier field).

 

Figure 3. 4      Qualifier Drop Down List

 

 

2.       Select Starting with from the drop down list.

 

Figure 3. 5      Starting With Selection

 

 

3.       In the search box enter the text for the concept you are searching on.

 

Figure 3. 6      Search Box (Starting with)

 

 

4.       To narrow your search to a particular category, click on the down arrow in the category selection box. Otherwise, accept the default of Any Category.

 

Figure 3. 7      Category Box

 

 

5.       Click on the Find button.

 

Figure 3. 8      Find Button

 

 

6.       The results will be displayed in the results section.

 

Figure 3. 9      Results for Search by Names
