Using Concepts

A concept can be added to other i2b2 views by simply dragging the concept name from the Workplace View to one of the following views.

 

Add Concept To Query Tool View

1.       Find the set of concepts you want to add (either in your folder or a shared folder).

 

Figure 9. 18    Workplace View

 

 

1.       Click on the concept and drag it over to the Query Tool View.

2.       Drop the concept into the panel labeled Group 1.

 

Figure 9. 19    Dropping Concept

 

 

3.       The concept will display.

 

Figure 9. 20    Concept in Group 1

 

 

Add Concept To Export Data View

1.       Find the set of concepts you want to add (either in your folder or a shared folder).

2.       Click on the concept and drag it over to the Export Data View.

3.       Drop the concept into the section that displays the list of concepts.

 

Figure 9. 21    Dropping Concept

 

 

4.       The concept will display.

 

Figure 9. 22    Concept List

 

 

Add Concept to Timeline View

1.       Find the set of concepts you want to add (either in your folder or a shared folder).

 

Figure 9. 23    Workplace View

 

 

2.       Click on the concept/term and drag the item over to the Timeline View.

3.       Drop the concept into the section that displays the list of concepts.

 

Figure 9. 24    Drag Concept

 

 

4.       The concept will display (see Figure 8.5).

 

Figure 9. 25    Concept Added
