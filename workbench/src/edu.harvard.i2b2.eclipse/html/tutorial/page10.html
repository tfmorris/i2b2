Constraining Items in the Group Panels

    Constrain by Date: Specify a date range for an item by clicking on 'Dates'

    Constrain by Occurrence: Specify minimum number of occurrences for an item by clicking on 'Occurs > 0x'

    Exclude Items: Specify the query criteria to be the inverse of the selected items by clicking on 'Exclude'

    Delete Item: Right click on item to remove from the Group Panel or select item and click on 'X'
