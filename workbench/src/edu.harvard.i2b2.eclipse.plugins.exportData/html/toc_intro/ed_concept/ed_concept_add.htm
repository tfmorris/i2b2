Add Concepts

Concepts can be added to the export data view by simply dragging the concept from one of the following three views.

 

1.       Navigate Terms

 

Figure 2.7

Navigate Terms View

 

 

2.       Find Terms

 

Figure 2.8

Find Terms View

 

 

3.       Workplace

 

Figure 2.9

Workplace View

 

 

From Navigate Terms View

1.       Expand the folders that contain the concept or grouping of concepts that you want to add.

2.       Click on the concept/term and drag the item over to the Export Data View.

3.       Drop the concept into the section that displays the list of concepts (figure 2.10).

 

Figure 2.10

Dropping Concept

 

 

4.       The concept will display (figure 2.11).

 

Figure 2.11

Concept List

 

 

From Find Terms View

1.       After searching for the term(s) you want to add, click on the concept/term and drag the item over to the Export Data View.

2.       Drop the concept into the section that displays the list of concepts (see figure 2.10).

3.       The concept will display (see figure 2.11).

 

From Workplace View

1.       Find the set of concepts you want to add (either in your folder or a shared folder).

2.       Click on the concept and drag it over to the Export Data View.

3.       Drop the concept into the section that displays the list of concepts (see figure 2.10)

4.       The concept will display (see figure 2.11).
